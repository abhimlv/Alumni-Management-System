SAKEC Alumni Portal

Shah and Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College was established in 1985 for the purpose of imparting quality technical education. The college is managed by Mahavir Education Trust. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and Government of Maharashtra, and is affiliated to University of Mumbai. It also has an ISO -9001 Certification. It offers under graduate courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Computer Science,Electronics & Telecommunication, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Cyber Security. It also offers Post Graduate Courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology & Electronics Engineering. This alumni portal is where the college, its alumni and students can interact with each other for their benefit. This portal offers the students an opportunity to contact the alumni, talk to them about the steps they should take to reach their goal, the mistakes they should avoid and what kind of opportunities to keep an eye out for. Alumni can nurture students and help them with their professional careers, giving them a head start.

Alumni likewise can scout for future prospects from the brilliant students who show time and again that they excel not only in their academics but also in extra-curricular activities. Other benefits for the alumni include being able to look for new and challenging jobs available on the portal. Alumni can support each other by sharing their knowledge, expertise and insight into cutting-edge research. This portal will give everyone access to a vast network of like-minded people which will only grow as new alumni join their ranks.

These portals also keep alumni updated about all the events going on in the institute. Alumni portals with its vast network also enhance the brand image of the institute. It showcases the rich community of the institute, and various initiatives taken that build positive impressions.

    Features of SAKEC Alumni Portal

  • Easy to use
  • Simple registration process for the Alumni
  • Registered alumni can edit text, images
  • Option to upload image files
  • Enrolled member list display in the system
  • Search option of alumni
  • Search can be year wise, address wise, name wise and branch wise
  • Form mail based communication method between the alumni
  • Admin option to delete alumni
  • Admin option to send common mailer to all alumni
